About TechUnravel

January Meetup (28th January, 2016)

Tech Unravel is a 1-day event for tech enthusiasts to discover and engage new technologies, and collaborate with one another.

The event seeks to bring people interested in developing their knowledge about the tech ecosystem in communities where TRIBE is found, and all across the world.

We hope to use this medium to gradually build our tech space, and develop communities.

Why join Tech Unravel January meetup?

Discover
- Learn Android and Firebase for Android
Engage
- Experience hands on sessions
Collaborate
- Work on some projects with interested developers

Our Goals

  • To build a tech community starting from the East
  • To connect developers from all works of life
  • To enlighten the surrounding community on the latest technology
