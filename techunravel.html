About Tech Unravel

Tech Unravel is a monthly meet up of tech enthusiasts.

Our focus is on discovering new technologies, engaging and collaborating with each other

It is a one day event where latest technology is discuss and break out sessions organized

The interested applicants of tech unravel will register before hand. technologies to discussed on the event will be shown on the site.

Why join Tech Unravel?

Discover
- Be up to date on the latest technology
Engage
- Experience hands on sessions
Collaborate
- Work on some projects with interested developers

Our Goals

  • To build a tech community starting from the East
  • To connect developers from all works of life
  • To enlighten the surrounding community on the latest technology
